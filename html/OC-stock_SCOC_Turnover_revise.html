Caption: Carbon stocks of (a) sediment (b) bacteria (c) meiofauna and (d) macrofauna of GC1 and GS1. The bar chart and error bars show the mean and standard deviation of carbon stocks in different sampling seasons. The dots represent the sum of OC stock (in (\(mg C/m^2\))) for each replicate. Note that summer sampling of sediment was only conducted once in OR1_1114. Bacteria was only collected in cruise OR1-1190. Meiofauna was only collected once in each seasons for the both sites, so the three replicates were conducted during the single cruise.
